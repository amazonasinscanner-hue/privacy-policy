Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Amazon Variation Scanner ("we", "our", or "the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

Summary: We collect your email address and license key to manage your subscription. We don't sell your data or track your browsing.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Collected during purchase to manage your subscription and send important updates about your account.
  • License Key: When you activate the Extension, your license key is stored locally in your browser and sent to our server for validation.
  • Instance ID: A unique identifier for your Extension installation, used to manage license activations.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your browsing history
  • Personal information from Amazon pages
  • Payment card information (handled securely by Lemon Squeezy)
  • Any data from websites other than amazon.com

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Validate your license and subscription status
  • Manage the number of devices using your license
  • Send important account notifications (subscription renewals, expiration, updates)
  • Provide customer support

We will NOT:

  • Sell your email address to third parties
  • Send spam or marketing emails without your consent
  • Share your personal information with advertisers

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • Locally: License key in your browser's extension storage (chrome.storage.local)
  • Server: Email and license information in our secure database
  • Lemon Squeezy: Payment and subscription data (see their privacy policy)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy: For payment processing and license management. View their Privacy Policy.
  • Railway: For hosting our license validation server.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • License keys are validated server-side
  • Webhook communications use cryptographic signatures
  • Database access is restricted and secured

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Deactivate: Remove your license from the Extension at any time
  • Unsubscribe: Cancel your subscription through Lemon Squeezy

7. Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
